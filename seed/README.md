## Business Model & Product Strategy (Launch Plan)

**Core Pillars (Evergreen + Emotional Buy):**
1. **ADHD / Neurodivergent Productivity Systems** — Dopamine menus, executive function planners, focus tools, habit systems tailored for ADHD brains.
2. **Women's Midlife Wellness Trackers** — Perimenopause/menopause symptom, mood, sleep, and hormone trackers (discreet, elegant, empowering — with strong disclaimers).
3. **Solopreneur & Creator Financial/Workflow Tools** — Clean money maps, content calendars, client trackers.
4. **Supporting Premium Wall Art & Affirmation Printables** — Calm, focus-oriented, neurodivergent-positive art that cross-sells beautifully.

**Pricing Strategy:**
- Singles: $7–$12
- Bundles (3–8 products): $15–$29
- Goal: Average order value $12–18. High perceived value through beautiful design + practical depth.

**Launch Goal:** 12–20 high-quality listings in first 7–14 days (using this system + Grok). Then 5–10 new or variations per week initially.

**Projected Ramp (Conservative, Organic Only):**
- Month 1: $300–$800 (learning + first sales)
- Month 3: $1,200–$2,500
- Month 6–12: $4,000–$12,000+/mo possible with 80–200 listings + strong Pinterest presence (realistic for dedicated operators in these niches).
